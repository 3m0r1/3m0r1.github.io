## Current State

Abyss C2 was meant to be a passion project for me to explore the offensive tooling world, but I've realized that developing a C2 solo is too difficult & time consuming.

I stopped working on Abyss C2 approximately 2 months ago, since then I've decided to try to work on smaller projects and expand my knowledge.

Before discontinuing Abyss, I had reached a solid milestone in its development, I had a good amount of features and a decently organized codebase.

I was also thinking about turning Abyss into a research project specifically focused on reverse engineering the windows usermode subsystem APIs to develop more sophisticated offensive toolings which would make Abyss C2 one of a kind. It would also help me understand security even better.

Unfortunately, progress started to decline after I had started thinking about the potential of this project and how much effort it would require for it to meet my high expectations.

The last thing I was working on was the plugin system Eclipse, I wanted users of Abyss C2 to have flexibility and freedom in their ability to model their own logic through use of events, commands, etc.


## The Future Of This Project

I plan on returning to Abyss C2 once I'm done with my other projects, I may also publish the current codebase on the the projects organization.
